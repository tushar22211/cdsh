Donation Policy

Donation is one of the easiest ways to help someone in need. The amount doesn’t have to be big – even a small donation can help in changing someone’s life in excellent ways. The Non-Governmental Organisations have been at the forefront to ensure that the ones in need are assisted for their issues.

NGOs and charitable trusts need funding to meet the expenses. Coins donated by the user in Cashdoot app is converted into money and transferred to organization selected by the user.

We take our donors privacy very seriously and ensure that their information is secured with us, and that, no third party source has access to them.

Cashdoot is not obliged to make refunds and in its discretion, declines any requests for refund of donations.
